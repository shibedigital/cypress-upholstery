Cypress Upholstery: Your Source for Custom Furniture in Vancouver

Cypress Upholstery is a family owned upholstery shop in Vancouver, British Columbia committed to customer service, quality and craftsmanship. We have over 35 years of experience in the industry.

We specialize in residential and commercial upholstery. We carry a wide range of fabrics in our showroom, in addition to foam and other materials such as springs and webbing.

Upholstery for Any Application

Whether you need upholstery for your antique furniture, sofa couch, kitchen chairs or need custom furniture built, we offer a wide range of services. We work with you throughout the process—from choosing your fabric, firmness of your foam and other small but important details. Contact us today to see how we can bring your furniture back to life!

Custom Reupholstering in Vancouver

At Cypress Upholstery, we do a wide range of services ranging from building customized furniture for residential and commercial set-ups to custom reupholstering of antique furniture in Vancouver.

Our upholstery services include:

  • Wall upholstery
  • Restyling and rebuilding
  • Antique refurbishing
  • Touch up of wood
  • Webbing added or replaced
  • New cushions (foam, down, springs)
  • Throw pillows
  • Arm caps
  • Chairs
  • Couches and sectionals
  • Restaurant benches
