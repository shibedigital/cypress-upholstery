Custom Reupholstering in Vancouver

At Cypress Upholstery, we do a wide range of services ranging from building customized furniture for residential and commercial set-ups to custom reupholstering of antique furniture in Vancouver.

Our upholstery services include:

  • Wall upholstery
  • Restyling and rebuilding
  • Antique refurbishing
  • Touch up of wood
  • Webbing added or replaced
  • New cushions (foam, down, springs)
  • Throw pillows
  • Arm caps
  • Chairs
  • Couches and sectionals
  • Restaurant benches
