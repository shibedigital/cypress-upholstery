Providing Fine Upholstery Service since 1998

Cypress Upholstery has been family owned and operated since 1998. What initially started as a business out of owner Mei Chor’s garage, Cypress Upholstery now resides on the south side of Vancouver. With its humble beginnings, Cypress Upholstery focuses on customer service, quality and craftsmanship.

At Cypress Upholstery, we bring your old or used furniture back to life! Apart from home decor, Cypress Upholstery also provides custom-built and sleek furniture for offices, restaurants, and various other commercial spaces.

With a satisfied clientele in and around the Vancouver, British Columbia region of Canada, Cypress is making its way among the top upholstery shops.

Mei Chor Yet

Mei Chor started Cypress Upholstery in 1998 after working in a trusted upholstery shop in Vancouver for 15 years. He has owned and operated Cypress Upholstery since the beginning.

Take a look at the host of furniture options and services below:

Antiques

We reupholster your antique furniture to maintain its natural beauty. We aim to decrease damage to antiques as well as improve the performance and functionality of it.

Cushions

We custom cut foam cushions for your couch, sofas, chairs, mattress and more. We also specialize in foam wedges for physiotherapists. Choose from a variety of foam thickness and firmness from our showroom! You’ll be sure to find one that suits your needs and liking.

Webbing and Springs Added or Replaced

We replace and add webbing or springs to your furniture.

Throw Pillows

We make custom throw pillows in a variety of shapes, patterns, colours, fabrics and sizes. Throw pillows are an affordable way to freshen up any room in your living space.

Wood Touch Up

We provide minor wood touch up on your furniture.

Kitchen and Dining Room Chairs

We specialize in the reupholstering and fixing of kitchen and dining room chairs. Whether you are looking to change or fix the fabric, foam, spring, or all of them, we have a wide selection of fabric and foam for you to choose from.

Wall Upholstery

We make custom headboards and upholstery for your wall panels to your specifications. You can choose wall upholstery for your residential or commercial projects.

Restaurant Benches, Chairs, Banquets

We reupholster and build benches, chairs, and banquets for restaurants. Whether you have an existing restaurant or are a contractor for a new restaurant, we can help!
